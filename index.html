About Us

GlobalAMZ is an agency focused on the Amazon online marketplace. We support manufacturers, retailers and brands with SEO-optimized content, optimal brand and product presentation, increasing sales and visibility, and strategic consulting.

As a young, dynamic team, we think in terms of flat hierarchies and offer plenty of creative freedom and development opportunities. We offer exciting, varied and challenging tasks, flexible working hours and remote working. Our modern office in the center of Munich (Goetheplatz) guarantees you a pleasant and motivating working environment.

Do you have Amazon complaints?

We have the right remedy

As the most influential online sales channel, Amazon poses particular challenges for the pharmaceutical industry (RX, OTC, food supplements): Strict regulations, complex listing requirements and monitoring all customer reactions. With our many years of expertise in the pharmaceutical sector, we can help you overcome these hurdles. From legally compliant product listings to customized marketing strategies, we offer you all the services you need to successfully use the Amazon sales channel and effectively position your products.

  • Own tool for pharmacovigilance compliance
  • Access to Amazon pharmacies
  • Adaptation, optimization and monitoring of pharma-related content with regard to all legal requirements and guidelines (HWG, NEM, OTC)
  • Independent, reliable, transparent

Amazon Expertise and Industry Knowledge:

GlobalAMZ has extensive experience in handling the Amazon marketplace, including all relevant strategies for optimal product presentation, PPC campaigns, and sales success. Specialized knowledge is particularly crucial in the complex Amazon environment to achieve maximum reach and sales.

Individual, Data-Driven Strategies:

GlobalAMZ develops tailored marketing strategies that are based on the specific needs of the customer and supported by in-depth analyses. This data-driven approach leads to more effective campaigns, higher conversion rates, and a measurable ROI.

Full-Funnel Amazon Marketing:

GlobalAMZ offers full-funnel approaches that cover all aspects of the sales process on Amazon, from brand awareness to customer loyalty and conversion. This ensures a consistent and efficient brand presence throughout the entire customer journey.

Experience with Complex Industries:

GlobalAMZ has special expertise in complex, highly regulated industries, such as the pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors, and understands the specific requirements and regulations of these markets. This industry knowledge enables them to provide tailored solutions that not only consider the Amazon algorithm but also address industry-specific challenges.
