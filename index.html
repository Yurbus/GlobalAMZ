About Us

GlobalAMZ is an agency focused on the Amazon online marketplace. We support manufacturers, retailers and brands with SEO-optimized content, optimal brand and product presentation, increasing sales and visibility, and strategic consulting.

As a young, dynamic team, we think in terms of flat hierarchies and offer plenty of creative freedom and development opportunities. We offer exciting, varied and challenging tasks, flexible working hours and remote working. Our modern office in the center of Munich (Goetheplatz) guarantees you a pleasant and motivating working environment.

Amazon Expertise and Industry Knowledge:

From legally compliant product listings to customized marketing strategies, we offer you all the services you need to successfully use the Amazon sales channel and effectively position your products.

  • Own tool for pharmacovigilance compliance
  • Access to Amazon pharmacies
