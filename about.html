About Us

GlobalAMZ is an Amazon-focused agency that supports manufacturers, retailers and brands in strategic planning, product presentation and sales growth. We offer specialized services such as process management, market monitoring and brand protection, both nationally and globally.

We translate our enthusiasm for Amazon into strong brand presence and sales, always with an eye on profitability and performance. With a broad customer base from various industries, we respond to different requirements in a targeted manner and are constantly evolving. Whether you are a newcomer or a globally active company - we take on every challenge. Let us convince you in a personal meeting.

The Story of GlobalAMZ: Our Journey to Amazon Expertise

GlobalAMZ was founded in response to a clear demand: with Amazon's rapid growth as a marketplace and marketing channel, many businesses and brands faced new challenges. Since our inception, we have grown steadily, specializing in Category Management, Performance Marketing, and Retail Marketing.

GlobalAMZ stands for innovation, expertise, and customized solutions for businesses looking to grow on Amazon. We’re ready for the next steps!

Connect
2019

Founding of GlobalAMZ

The idea for GlobalAMZ emerged from the successful seller activities of our founder and CEO, Gregor Leopold. Many business partners sought his advice and expertise in navigating Amazon, which led to the creation of the agency.

2020

Focus on Pharma

During the agency's development, a strong focus on the pharmaceutical and cosmetics sectors emerged, where we now offer tailored solutions.

2021

New Office at Goetheplatz

With a growing team, GlobalAMZ moved into a new office at Goetheplatz in Munich. After the pandemic, we embraced a return to normal working life, creating a space for collaboration and creativity.

2022

Amazon Ads Partnership

A major milestone was achieving Amazon Ads Partner status, solidifying our position as a leading agency for performance marketing and further strengthening our expertise.

2024

Continued Growth

The expert team at GlobalAMZ continues to expand, having more than doubled in size since its founding. With a strong team, we are driving our success story forward.
