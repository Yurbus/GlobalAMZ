About Us

GlobalAMZ is an Amazon-focused agency that supports manufacturers, retailers and brands in strategic planning, product presentation and sales growth. We offer specialized services such as process management, market monitoring and brand protection, both nationally and globally.

We translate our enthusiasm for Amazon into strong brand presence and sales, always with an eye on profitability and performance. With a broad customer base from various industries, we respond to different requirements in a targeted manner and are constantly evolving. Whether you are a newcomer or a globally active company - we take on every challenge. Let us convince you in a personal meeting.
